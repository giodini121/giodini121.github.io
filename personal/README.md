# Assets for this site

Place these files here:

1. **`profile.jpg`** — Sidebar photo (square or headshot; displayed at max 120px width).
2. **`academic_cv.pdf`** — Your CV PDF (used on the CV page for embed and download).

Until `profile.jpg` exists, the browser may show a broken image icon. Until `academic_cv.pdf` exists, the CV page preview will be empty—use **Download** after you add the file.

You can copy an official CV PDF from your [University of Bologna CV page](https://www.unibo.it/sitoweb/giorgio.dini2/en) if you publish one there.
