About

I am a research fellow at the Department of Economics, University of Bologna. I am completing my PhD within the European Doctorate in Law and Economics (EDLE), a joint programme across Bologna, Rotterdam, and Hamburg.

My research focuses on human behaviour in environmental and climate challenges, using tools from experimental economics (lab experiments, online studies, and field work). I am part of the ERC-funded Green Tipping project led by Prof. Alessandro Tavoni.

I have been a visiting research collaborator at Princeton University (2024) and at Erasmus University Rotterdam (2021–2022). I hold an MSc in Resource Economics and Sustainable Development and a BSc in Business Administration from the University of Genoa.
