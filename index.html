About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Economics, University of Bologna, as I am part of the ERC-funded Green Tipping project led by Prof. Alessandro Tavoni. In Bologna I completed my PhD in Economics within the European Doctorate in Law and Economics (EDLE), a joint programme across Bologna, Rotterdam, and Hamburg.

My research focuses on human behaviour in environmental and climate challenges, using tools from experimental economics (lab, online, and field experiments).

I am part of the Youth Scholar Group of the Beijer Institute for Ecological Economics since 2024 (current Beijer Young Scholars).

I visited Princeton University in 2024 (Sep–Dec) and Erasmus University Rotterdam in 2021–2022 (Sep–Mar). I hold an MSc in Resource Economics and Sustainable Development and a BSc in Business Administration from the University of Genoa.
